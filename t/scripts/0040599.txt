Basaloid_monomorphic_adenomas._Monomorphic_adenomas_can_be_divided_into_basaloid_and_nonbasaloid_type._With_the_exception_of_the_canalicular_adenoma,_the_basaloid_monomorphic_adenomas_are_preponderantly_tumors_of_the_major_salivary_glands,_most_often_the_parotid_gland._The_dermal_analogue_monomorphic_adenomas,_so_named_because_of_their_histologic_similarity_to_eccrine_tumors_of_the_skin,_are_a_unique_subset_of_the_basaloid_adenomas._They_are_often_multifocal,_recur_frequently,_may_undergo_malignant_change,_and_can_be_part_of_a_salivary-cutaneous_tumor_diathesis.
