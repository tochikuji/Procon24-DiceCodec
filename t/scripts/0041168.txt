_100_or_more_keratotic_skin_lesions_(relative_risk,_4.8;_95_percent_confidence_interval,_1.5_to_15.1)._CONCLUSIONS._HLA-B_mismatching_is_significantly_associated_with_the_risk_of_squamous-cell_carcinoma_in_renal-transplant_recipients,_as_is_HLA-DR_homozygosity._An_indirect_effect_on_the_level_of_immunosuppression_does_not_appear_to_explain_these_findings,_nor_does_exposure_to_sunlight_or_the_number_of_keratotic_skin_lesions_account_for_this_observation.
